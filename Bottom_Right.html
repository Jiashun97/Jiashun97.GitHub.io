Research Interests

My current research interests lie in using computational models (i.e. Drift-Diffusion Models, DDM) to study human decision making under urgency. I am also working on a brain imaging project in which I am trying to build a joint model of EEG and DDM to investigate change of mind in decision making. Furthermore, I have a general interest in applying DDM to practical problems, such as in clinical settings and in the field of learning science.
